Anatomy of a liquid pane

Inner shadows carve a concave bowl; a masked gradient paints the specular rim; a ::after layer renders cursor light via custom properties; and a diagonal sheen — the caustic — passes over on hover. Four stacked illusions, zero images.

Every panel on this page shares one primitive. Depth comes from restraint, not from stacking blur on blur.
