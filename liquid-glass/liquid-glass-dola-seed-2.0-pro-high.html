Liquid Glass

An interface that behaves like the surface of perfectly still water. Soft, viscous, and alive.

Surface Tension

Each element rests on the liquid surface, distorting it gently. Light refracts through the glass, creating soft gradients and subtle depth. Nothing is flat, nothing is static.

Interaction

Click anywhere on the surface to create ripples that propagate across the entire interface. All motion is deliberately slow, viscous, and calm. This is not the sharp, snappy motion of digital interfaces. This is the motion of physical matter.
